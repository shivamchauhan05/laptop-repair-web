Screen Repair at a Glance

Common Screen Problems We Fix

  • Cracked, shattered, or damaged display panels
  • Flickering, flashing, or dim screen issues
  • Backlight problems (screen stays dark)
  • Lines, distortion, or no image (image/connector failure)
  • Loose or damaged display cables (hinge/flex cable)
  • Touchscreen display issues (if applicable)

What to Expect

  1. Screen inspection to confirm the cause (panel vs. cable vs. backlight)
  2. Repair or replacement with a compatible, high-quality screen
  3. Testing for brightness, resolution, color, touch (if applicable), and stability
