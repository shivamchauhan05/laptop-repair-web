Signs Your Laptop Needs a New Battery

Several warning signs may indicate that your laptop battery requires replacement

Rapid Battery Drain

Battery percentage drops quickly even with light usage.

Difficulty Charging

Laptop doesn't charge or takes unusually long to charge.

Unexpected Power Loss

Laptop shuts down suddenly even showing remaining charge.

Battery Swelling

Visible bulging on laptop chassis or touchpad becomes hard to press.

Overheating

Bottom of laptop becomes unusually hot during charging.

Battery Health Warnings

System shows "replace battery" or "battery not detected".

If you notice any of these issues, our technicians can inspect your laptop and recommend the most suitable solution.
