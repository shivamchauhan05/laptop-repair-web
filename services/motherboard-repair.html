Frequently Asked Questions

How long does motherboard repair take?
Standard repairs take 2–3 days. Express service available (24h) for an additional fee. Complex board repairs may take up to 5 days.
Do you offer warranty on motherboard repairs?
Yes, we provide 6 months warranty on all soldering and component-level repairs. Free rework if same issue recurs.
Can you recover data from a dead motherboard?
Usually yes — we first repair power delivery to retrieve data. If the SSD/HDD is functional, data remains intact.
Do you repair all laptop brands?
Absolutely. Dell, HP, Lenovo, Acer, ASUS, Apple MacBook, MSI, and more. We have donor boards and safe procedures for major brands.
