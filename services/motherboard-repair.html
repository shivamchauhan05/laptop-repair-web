Frequent Motherboard Failures

We diagnose and resolve complex hardware faults with advanced tools and certified technicians.

No Power or Dead Laptop

If your laptop won’t turn on, the motherboard may have damaged power circuits, faulty MOSFETs, or shorted components. We run detailed diagnostics and restore proper power delivery.

Black Screen and No Display

Display failures are often caused by motherboard faults affecting graphics processing, display controllers, or signal circuits. We diagnose accurately and repair at the component level.

Overheating and Sudden Shutdowns

Excessive heat can damage voltage regulation circuits and critical motherboard components. We repair VRM sections, replace damaged parts, and improve stability for reliable performance.

Liquid Damage Repair

Spilled liquids can cause corrosion and shorts. Our advanced cleaning and repair process removes corrosion, restores motherboard traces, and replaces damaged components.

Charging, USB, HDMI, Audio & Connectivity Issues

We fix charging circuit faults and battery detection issues, along with USB, HDMI, audio, and connectivity failures caused by damaged controllers, connectors, or circuit pathways.

BIOS and Firmware Corruption

Boot failures, system crashes, and startup errors are often caused by corrupted BIOS firmware. We provide professional BIOS programming and recovery services.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can a motherboard be repaired?
Yes. Most motherboard failures can be repaired through component-level diagnostics and targeted replacement or micro-soldering.
How long does motherboard repair take?
Most repairs are completed within 1 to 3 business days, depending on the complexity. Express turnaround may be available.
Is motherboard repair cheaper than replacement?
In many cases, yes. Component-level repairs are typically more cost-effective than replacing the entire motherboard.
Will my data be safe during repair?
Yes. Repairs generally do not affect data stored on your SSD/HDD. If you need data recovery, we can help separately.
Do you repair liquid-damaged motherboards?
Absolutely. We perform liquid damage diagnostics, corrosion cleaning, trace repair, and component replacement where needed.
Do you provide repair warranties?
Yes. Warranty coverage is available on eligible motherboard repairs and replacement components.
Can you repair gaming laptop motherboards?
Yes. We repair gaming laptops from brands like MSI, Alienware, ASUS ROG, Acer Predator, and more.
What causes motherboard failure?
Common causes include overheating, liquid damage, power surges, component aging, and manufacturing defects.
